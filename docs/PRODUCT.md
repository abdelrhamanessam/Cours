# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

Primary and secondary school students (ages 8–18) looking for structured, premium math learning. Secondary audience: parents who evaluate and purchase tutoring for their children. The surface speaks to students in the learning flow and to parents on the landing page.

## Product Purpose

Mr Maths makes elite 1-on-1 math tutoring accessible online. Interactive lessons, adaptive quizzes, and progress tracking — all guided by Issam Edris's teaching method. Students build confidence through precision. Success looks like a student independently progressing through lessons, improving scores, and returning daily.

## Positioning

Premium 1-on-1 math mastery — Issam Edris brings elite tutoring online with confidence through precision.

## Brand Personality

Premium, confident, precise. The voice is encouraging but never childish — clear instructions, warm acknowledgment, direct feedback. The feel is that of an expert tutor who respects the student's intelligence.

## Anti-references

Not childish or cartoonish. No bright primary colors, no mascots, no gamification gimmicks. The interface should feel like a premium learning tool — confident, clean, and serious about results.

## Design Principles

1. **Confidence through clarity** — Every screen reduces cognitive load. Students never wonder what to do next.
2. **Precision in every pixel** — Tight spacing, sharp typography, deliberate color. Nothing is accidental.
3. **Earned delight** — Motion and polish serve comprehension and achievement, not decoration.
4. **Respect the student** — The interface treats learners as capable. Explanations are precise, not patronizing.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA minimum. Body text contrast ≥4.5:1. All interactive elements focus-visible. Reduced motion respected. Clear error feedback on quizzes and forms.
